About Us

Who we are

New Dawn Cleaning is family run company serving the Lower Mainland of B.C. Our goal is to make your home, business, or site sparkle. We want to help our clients save time for the things that matter, like spending time with loved ones or catching up on your favorite show. The utmost care and attention in our work keeps customers coming back time after time. There are a range of services to choose from. Such as one time cleans, weekly, bi-weekly and monthly cleans!
